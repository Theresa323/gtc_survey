Well done - you now get to play the game "Guess the City".

The following section explains how the game works. Please read the instructions carefully, as your financial compensation will depend upon your performance.

You will be shown different pictures from Google Streetview that you need to classify according to their city of origin:

  • Berlin (Germany)
  • Hamburg (Germany)
  • Tel Aviv (Israel)
  • Jerusalem (Israel)

Doing this, you play against an Artificial Intelligence (AI) which performs the same tasks. After having made your choice for each picture you will see how the AI has decided.

You will play 10 rounds against the AI. Good luck!
